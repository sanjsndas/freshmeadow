    ❓ Frequently asked questions

    Can I substitute whole wheat flour?

    Yes – but you may need to adjust hydration. We recommend starting with a 50/50 blend.

    How do I store sourdough starter?

    Keep it in the fridge and feed weekly. For long breaks, dry or freeze it.

    Are your recipes gluten‑free adaptable?

    Many are – look for the GF tag. We also have dedicated gluten‑free guides.
