Cookies policy

We use cookies to enhance your experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

📅 Last updated: April 2025 ✅ GDPR / ePrivacy compliant 🔒 Privacy-first approach

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you interact with our content, and improve your overall experience. Cookies are not used to personally identify you.

2. How we use cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential: to enable core functionality like security, network management, and accessibility.
  • Performance & analytics: to understand how visitors use our site, so we can improve content and usability.
  • Preferences: to remember your settings, such as language or region.
  • Marketing (optional): to deliver relevant content and offers, based on your browsing behavior.

4. Your consent & control

When you first visit our site, we ask for your consent to set non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer. You can also manage or delete cookies directly in your browser settings.

Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of the site.

5. Third-party cookies

We may use services like Google Analytics and social media platforms that set their own cookies. These are governed by the respective third party's privacy policy. We do not control these cookies, but we limit their use to essential analytics.

6. Updates to this policy

We may revise this Cookies Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify you via a banner or email if significant. Your continued use of our site indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@freshmeadow.com.
