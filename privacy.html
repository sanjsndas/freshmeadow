🔒 privacy & trust

Your privacy matters

At Fresh Meadow Meals, we are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

📅 Last updated: April 2025 ✅ GDPR compliant

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as when you subscribe to our newsletter, fill out a contact form, or comment on a recipe. This may include your name, email address, and any message content.

We also automatically collect certain data via cookies and similar technologies, including IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This helps us improve your experience.

2. How we use your information

  • To communicate: respond to inquiries, send newsletters, and notify you about updates.
  • To improve: analyze usage patterns to enhance our content and design.
  • To protect: detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents.

3. Sharing & disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share information with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., email platforms, analytics) who process data on our behalf, under strict confidentiality agreements.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Fresh Meadow Meals, our users, or others.

4. Data security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption, firewalls, and access controls, to safeguard your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You can also withdraw consent for marketing communications. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@freshmeadow.com.

6. Cookies & tracking

We use cookies to remember preferences, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You can control cookie settings in your browser. For more details, see our Cookies Policy.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a prominent notice on this page. Your continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@freshmeadow.com.
